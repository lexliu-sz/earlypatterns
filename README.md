# EarlyPatterns

Speak or type a 30-second note whenever something happens — a meltdown, a refusal, a calm win. On-device speech recognition transcribes instantly. Each entry captures what triggered it, how intense it was, and how it resolved. Over time the app surfaces repeating patterns and lets you export structured ABC-framework reports — Antecedent, Behaviour, Consequence — ready for meetings with teachers, SENCOs, or paediatricians. Everything stays on your device, private and encrypted.

iPhone & iPad. Free. No ads, no in-app purchases, no accounts, no network.
